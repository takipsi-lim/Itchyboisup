"Historya!"

At the heart of "Historya" is the powerful message that we are not truly alone, even in moments of isolation. It’s a song for the dreamers,
the lost souls, the ones who walk in silence yet still hold on to the belief that their journey is worth something. Supsrem’s poignant lyrics speak to anyone who has ever felt forgotten or overlooked, reminding us all that our personal history is worth telling, and that there is always hope in the struggle.

Who is Supsrem?

"Lim Gloria"

Supsrem (born on September 3, 2006, in the City of San Jose del Monte, Bulacan, Philippines) is a Filipino singer, songwriter, and producer known for his introspective lyricism, unique genre-blending sound, and raw emotional expression.

As an independent artist, Supsrem is recognized for combining elements of hip-hop,
R&B, pop, and electronic music to create emotionally resonant songs that connect
deeply with his audience.
